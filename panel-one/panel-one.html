Cupcake Recipes

Lemon

  • 1/2 cup unsalted softened butter
  • 1 cup granulated white sugar
  • 2 large eggs, at room temperature
  • 1 & 1/2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1 & 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1 & 1/2 tablespoons lemon zest
  • 1/3 fresh lemon juice

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Beat butter and sugar together on medium-high speed, then add eggs and vanilla until combined
  3. In a seperate bowl, mix flour, baking powder, and salt together
  4. Mix dry and wet ingredients together, and then add milk, lemon juice, and lemon zest
  5. Pour into 12-cup muffin pan and bake for 18 minutes

Funfetti

  • 1 & 2/3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup granulated white sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/4 cup yogurt
  • 3/4 cup whole milk
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup rainbow sprinkles

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Mix together flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Then melt butter in microwave and stir in sugar
  3. After the butter and sugar mixture has cooled, stir in egg, yogurt, milk, and vanilla extract
  4. Mix in dry ingredients and then gently fold in sprinkles
  5. Pour into 12-cup muffin pan and bake for 18 minutes

Chocolate

  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened natural cocoa powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs, at room temperature
  • 1/2 cup granulated white sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 1/3 vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk, at room temperature

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Mix together flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt
  3. In a seperate bowl, mix together eggs, granulated sugar, brown sugar, oil, and vanilla until combined
  4. Then, pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stream in buttermilk
  5. Pour into 12-cup muffin pan and bake for 18 minutes
